It’s that time of year again where we all convene in San Francisco for the AGU Fall Meeting!

Check out our line-up of oral and poster presentations.

Current Members

Keita DeCarlo and Kelly Caylor, H13K-1573 – Linking Carbon Flux Dynamics and Soil Structure in Dryland Soils, Poster, Monday, 12 December 2016, 13:40 – 18:00, Moscone South, Poster Hall.

Cynthia Gerlein-Safdi, Taking Up the Torch: Emerging Issues in Tropical Ecohydrology from Early Career Scientists, Pop-Up Talk, Tuesday, December 14, 16:00 – 16:10, Moscone West, 2001A.

Cynthia Gerlein-Safdi, Steve Frolking, and Kelly Caylor , B33F-0681 – Characterization of canopy dew formation in tropical forests using active microwave remote sensing, Poster, Wednesday, 14 December 2016, 13:40 – 18:00, Moscone South, Poster Hall.

Drew Gower , Paul McCord, Kelly Caylor , Jampel Dell’Angelo, and Tom Evans. H33E-1581 – An ecohydrologic framework for simulating catchment constraints on smallholder irrigation systems in drylands, Poster, Wednesday, 14 December 2016, 13:40 – 18:00, Moscone South, Poster Hall.

 

Alumni and Collaborators

Tom Evans, Lyndon Estes , Kelly Caylor , Paul McCord, Drew Gower , Megan Konar, Katherine Baylis, Kurt Waldman, Jordan Blekking, and Tyler Schlachter, OS14A-05 – Harmonizing Social and Environmental Dynamics in Earth Systems Modeling, Oral Presentation, Monday, 12 December 2016, 17:00 – 17:15, Moscone West, 3011.

Marshaun Montgomery, Katherine Baylis, and Tom Evans. PA14B-07 – A Framework for Using Rural Markets to Analyze Local Food Shortage Resilience and Mitigation Potential in sub-Saharan Africa based on Evidence from Zambia, Oral Presentation, Monday, 12 December 2016, 17:30 – 17:45, Moscone South, 309.

Abinash Bhattachan , Todd BenDor, Marcelo Ardón, Emily Bernhardt, Justin Wright, and Ryan Samuel, GC23F-1294 – Re-plumbing the coast: Untangling the effects of climate change and water management on vulnerability of coastal landscapes to saltwater incursion, Poster, Tuesday, 13 December 2016, 13:40 – 18:00, Moscone South, Poster Hall.

Stephen Good , John Selker, Firas Al-Qqaili, Manuel Lopez, and Lauren Kahel, H33E-1590 – Modular 3D-Printed Soil Gas Probes, Poster, Wednesday, 14 December 2016, 13:40 – 18:00, Moscone South, Poster Hall.

Andreas Colliander, Thomas Jackson, Rajat Bindlish, Steven Chan, Narendra Das, Seungbum Kim, Michael Cosh, Scott Dunbar, Jun Asanuma, Kentaro Aida, Aaron Berg, Tracy Rowlandson, David Bosch, Todd Caldwell, Kelly Caylor, David Goodrich, Hala Khalid Al Jassar, Ernesto Lopez-baeza, Jose Martínez-Fernández, Angel Gonzales-Zamora, Stanley Livingston, Heather McNairn, Anna Pacheco, Mahta Moghaddam, Carsten Montzka, Claudia Notarnicola, Georg Niedrist, Thierry Pellarin, John Prueger, Jouni Pulliainen, Kimmo Rautiainen, Judith Ramos, Mark Seyfried, Patrick Starks, Zhongbo Su, Yijan Zeng, Rogier Van der Velde, Marc Thibeault, Wouter Dorigo, Mariette Vreugdenhil, Jeffrey Walker, Xiaoling Wu, Alessandra Monerris, Peggy O’Neill, Dara Entekhabi, and Simon Yueh, B33J-04 – Assessment of SMAP L2/L3 Soil Moisture Products using In Situ Based Core Validation Sites, Oral Presentation, Wednesday, 14 December 2016, 14:25 – 14:40, Moscone West, 2008.

Trenton Franz , EP43E-01 – Design of multiscale soil water content monitoring networks, Oral Presentation, Thursday, 15 December 2016, 13:40 – 13:55, Moscone West, 2003. 

Tom Evans, Kelly Caylor , Lyndon Estes , Beth Plale, Shahzeen Attari, and Kurt Waldman, GC52C-01 – Rapid Monitoring of Drought Impacts on Small-Scale Farms in Africa through Integration of Farmer SMS data and Environmental Sensors, Oral Presentation, Friday, 16 December 2016, 10:20 – 10:35, Moscone West, 3003.

Zhenzhong Zeng, Di Tian, Lyndon Estes , Tom Evans, Eric Wood, and Kelly Caylor , GC53E-1347 – Can we forecast farmers’ yields? The relationships between rainfall variability, farmers’ expectations, and actual yields in a tropical dryland, Poster, Friday, 16 December 2016, 13:40 – 18:00, Moscone South, Poster Hall.

Lyndon Estes , Stephanie Debats , Kelly Caylor , Tom Evans, Drew Gower , Dennis McRitchie, Tim Searchinger, David Thompson, Eric Wood, and Lindy Zeng. IN54A-07 – Combining Human and Machine Learning to Map Cropland in the 21st Century’s Major Agricultural Frontier, Oral Presentation, Friday, 16 December 2016, 17:30 – 17:45, Moscone West, 2000.
